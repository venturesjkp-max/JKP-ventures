About JKP Ventures

Building sustainable IP assets that generate long-term value through strategic partnerships

JKP Ventures FZ-LLC is a Dubai-based Free Zone company that develops, owns, and licenses software and data-analytics platforms globally.

We manage proprietary intellectual property, digital infrastructure, and consulting frameworks used by research and technology organisations worldwide.

Our focus is on building and maintaining long-term IP assets that generate sustainable value through licensing and technology partnerships.

Headquartered in Dubai Silicon Oasis, we leverage the UAE's strategic position to serve clients across the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and the Americas.
